Our Story

Fresh Bread, Haitian Flavor, Family Hospitality

Founded on November 24, 2018 by Oswald Gaboyau and Marjorie Gaboyau, Seven Wonders began as a small Haitian bakery and grew into a full-service Haitian & Caribbean restaurant loved across Jacksonville.

Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and the homemade flavors that remind guests of home.
